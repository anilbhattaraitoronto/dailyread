Left for Post-post-politics

Anil Bhattarai

November 19, 2018, Monday

According to Chantal Mouffe, the current 'populist moment' offer possibilities for left to reinvent the radical democratic politics.For that to become reality, left need to reinvent itself in some profound ways

Mouffe draws a lot from Antonio Gramsci in making her arguments. She also adds her own insights. According to her, left politics can seize on this populist moment only if they abandon two of the main pillars of left thinking: one, the idea that radical politics and liberal democracy do not go together; two, that working class is the primary agent of social transformation. According to her, these two ideological obsessions have paralized the left for over three decades. In fact, for Mouffe, the guide to action for the left can come from what the Right did during the last decades.
